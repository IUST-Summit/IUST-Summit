Submission Procedure

  • Authors should submit Full papers, not just an Abstract.
  • Only papers submitted through CMT will be accepted.
  • Paper Formatting and Margins: Please carefully follow the template for specifications.
  • No page numbers please. The same will be inserted during proceedings.
  • The Similarity Index should be below 20%.
  • Springer encourage authors of proceedings to include their ORCIDs in the proceedings.
  • Please ensure that all the figures are of 300 DPI resolutions.
