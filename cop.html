CALL FOR PAPERS

The First International Conference of IUST on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Intelligent Systems (ICAIMLIS2023) aims to establish a unique platform for the exchange of knowledge among researchers and practitioners in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Intelligent Systems. We extend a warm invitation to researchers and practitioners from across the globe who are engaged in advancing theory, methodology, and applications in these domains to contribute their research articles to the conference. We encourage submissions in these focal areas, as well as other pertinent subjects. Authors are encouraged to submit original, unpublished, and high-quality research papers (full-length manuscripts) that elucidate theoretical frameworks, methodologies, and applications showcasing noteworthy progress in the following key areas
