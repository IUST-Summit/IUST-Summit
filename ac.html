WORKSHOPS

Workshops are typically a part of AI conferences, and are designed to provide a more in-depth and hands-on learning experience for attendees. Workshops provide attendees with practical knowledge and skills they can apply to their own projects. Workshops include interactive sessions and demonstrations and provide opportunities for attendees to network with industry experts and other professionals. The Conference shall focus on following two workshops.

  • Handling Imbalanced Datasets in Deep Learning
  • Deep Reinforcement Learning workshops
